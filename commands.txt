Fylrobot commands for CollabVM:

!help - Help.
!spam - Spams a phrase in chat.
!rapidname - Changes name rapidly.
!custommessage - Makes Fylrobot say a custom message.
!screenshot - Takes screenshot of VM.
!turnspam - Spams turn.
!disconnect - Reloads page.
!record - Record VM screen.
!customscript - Add a script from a URL.
!darkmode - Adds dark mode to your screen. (Made by @netflixcvm on repl.it, or netflix on CollabVM.)
!autotype - Autotypes a phrase inside the VM. (also made by Netflix.)
